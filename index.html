Our Story





9 years ago...

Letorta has been serving its customers with yummy delightful cakes and bakery products since 2014.We are located in Kochi, which is a popular travel destination among tourists. We specialise in producing moist, delicious cakes on a large scale that have ‘baked at home taste.Cake Orders can be placed online making Letorta just a click away. We are famous for the variety of cakes we provide, we do not compromise on the quality of our Cakes. We are Largest Online Cake Shop in Kochi, specialising in traditional homemade cakes, cookies and brownies and sell them in the local area. We have put our heart and soul in to creating & produce cakes with a homemade look and taste.




"Our passion drives us to experiment everyday "



Chef with a difference

Letorta only uses the finest ingredients in its products, ensuring the goods that we send out into the market are of the utmost quality. Letorta believes in order to give the Real taste of any product, real ingredients should be used & not cheaper substitutes. Our production includes a highly-customized, automated system but does not forego the importance of the human touch, while still adhering to strict quality control measures at every stage of production, packaging, and delivery.
